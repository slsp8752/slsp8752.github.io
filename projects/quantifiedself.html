Data Driven Immersive Theatre - Quantified Self

Fall 2015 - Spring 2016

Quantified Self was an immersive theatre experience with no boundary between the audience and the performance. I helped design games that used the audience's social media data to challenge them on the ethics of data collection and use. I also worked on many of the physical set pieces, and helped out with troubleshooting during the show.
