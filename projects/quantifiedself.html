Data Driven Immersive Theatre - Quantified Self

Fall 2015 - Spring 2016

I helped pilot a data driven immersive theater piece put together by Mike Skirpan. I designed games that used the audience's social media data to challenge them on the ethics of data collection and use. I also worked on many of the physical set pieces, and helped out with troubleshooting during the show.
