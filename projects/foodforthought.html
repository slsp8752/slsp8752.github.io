Food For Thought - Volunteer Organization App

Fall 2016-Spring 2017

For my CS senior project, I worked on the front end of a multiplatform app to manage volunteers for the non profit organization Food For Thought. We used the Ionic Framework to use one codebase for iOS, Android, and the Web.

As the final product was incomplete, our code was handed off to another team. So while you can't test the app out, you can check out a demo of some of the features in the video below!
