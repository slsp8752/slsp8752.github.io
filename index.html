Hi, I'm Slaton Spangler

I am an artist, computer scientist, and tinkerer from Boulder, CO. I've recently graduated with a BS in Computer Science from CU-Boulder, and am working on various freelance and personal projects.

I have experience with both hardware and software, which has allowed me to help pilot a data-driven theater project, explore the technology surrounding aerial photogrammetry, create a motion controlled interactive sign, and construct a 'thank you' button that wildly flashes a light miles away. I'm always looking for more projects to test my skills and spark my creativity!

Aerial Photogrammetry

As a part of CU-Boulder's 2015 Grand Challenge, I helped create a system to demonstrate the use of UAVs to map terrain.

Quantified Self

I helped create an immersive theatre experience by building set pieces and programming data driven games.

The BTU Lab

The BTU Lab (or Blow Things Up Lab) is a Hackerspace on the CU Boulder campus. I work as the lab assistant, helping out members with their projects and creating projects to bring attention to the lab.

FoodForThought App

For my Computer Science 'Senior Projects' course, I helped design a volunteer organization app for a non-profit organization.
