Hi, I'm Slaton Spangler

I am an artist, computer scientist, and tinkerer. I've worked on tons of weird techy projects over the years and I don't plan to stop anytime soon.

I have experience with both hardware and software, which has allowed me to help pilot a data-driven theater project, create a motion controlled interactive sign, construct a 'thank you' button that wildly flashes a light miles away, and create puzzles and art for Meow Wolf.

You can explore the various projects I've worked on below. Feel free to get in touch with me via email at srs965[at]gmail.com
